ACUTE RISK LEVEL

Emergency Services

Call emergency services immediately (911 or the equivalent in your area) for urgent assistance

Stay with Them

Remain with the person to provide support and prevent any further harm. Do not leave them alone.

Remove Means of Harm

Safely remove any objects that could be used for self-harm or suicide from the immediate environment.

Listen and Validate

Offer a listening ear without judgment. Validate their feelings and let them know they are not alone.
